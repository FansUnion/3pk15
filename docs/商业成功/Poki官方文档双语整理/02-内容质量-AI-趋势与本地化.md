## Quality Guidelines — 总述

**English:** Poki prioritizes games proven interesting to its audience—original, high-quality titles that add something new to web games. The platform is highly curated and reserves the right to reject content.

**中文：** Poki 优先已验证受玩家欢迎、原创且高质量、能为网页游戏带来新意的作品。平台高度策展，保留拒稿权利。

---

## What Poki loves to see

**English:**

- **Originality & uniqueness** — Fresh ideas or creative twists; clear differentiation in saturated genres (see Trends if trend-inspired).
- **Web-first** — Designed for web, not a mobile port.
- **Polished presentation** — Visual cohesion, pleasant animation, consistent responsive UX, thoughtful feedback loops.
- **Wholesome & inclusive** — Family-friendly themes for all ages and backgrounds.

**中文：**

- **原创与差异** — 新玩法或熟悉机制上的创意变体；饱和品类中要有明显区分（趋势向作品另见 Trends）。
- **Web 优先** — 为网页设计，而非手游移植。
- **完成度** — 视觉统一、动画舒适、UX 一致响应、反馈循环合理。
- **健康包容** — 全年龄段、全背景友好的家庭向主题。

---

## Accessibility and alternative controls

**English:** Not all players can use WASD-style keyboard input. Where possible, support mouse-only, drag steering, auto acceleration, or remappable keys. Flexible input materially expands audience and fairness.

**中文：** 并非所有玩家都能用 WASD 等键盘操作。尽量支持纯鼠标、拖拽转向、自动加速或可改键。灵活输入能显著扩大受众并提升公平性。

---

## What Poki will not work with

**English:**

- **Unsafe themes** — Bullying, abuse, stereotypes, sexism, graphic violence, sustained horror, sexual content, inappropriate dress, cheating, gambling, alcohol, tobacco, etc.
- **Unlicensed IP / heavy clones** — No direct copies; template/asset reuse with little creativity; games too close to known IP in art, themes, modes, concept. Inspired-by-genre games OK if they add something new. See clone vs inspired table below.
- **Low quality** — Bugs, excessive load size, poor performance, confusing UX, inconsistent graphics. Extra care with AI (see Working With AI).

**Violations:** Responsive developers may fix and resubmit after **significant** improvement (similarity → innovate; mild inappropriate content → remove/reframe; unpolished → polish). **Immediate rejection:** IP misuse, adult themes. Strong performance does **not** override guideline violations; detailed rejection reasons are not always provided.

**中文：**

- **不安全主题** — 欺凌、虐待、刻板印象、性别歧视、Graphic 暴力、持续恐怖、性内容、不当着装、作弊、赌博、烟酒等。
- **未授权 IP / 高度克隆** — 禁止一比一复制、模板/asset 几乎不改、整体酷似知名 IP。借鉴品类可以，须有新贡献。
- **低质量** — Bug、过大加载、性能差、UX 混乱、美术不一致；用 AI 时尤其谨慎。

**违规后：** 积极响应、**显著**改进或可再议；**IP 滥用、成人主题**直接拒。表现好也不能豁免；拒因未必详述。

## Clones vs inspired games

**English:**

| Dimension | ❌ Clone | ✅ Inspired |
|-----------|----------|-------------|
| Art style | Nearly identical colors, shading, themes | Stylistically different or re-themed (sci-fi, underwater, etc.) |
| Core loop | Same loop, goals, inputs unchanged | Unique mechanic, twist, meta layer, genre blend |
| Progression / economy | Same pacing, structure, monetization | Original flow; adjusted monetization |
| UI / audio / font | Copied layout, palette, icons, cues | Distinct interface and custom assets |
| Characters | Same designs, silhouettes, animations | Original characters, no IP infringement |
| Name / thumbnail | SEO-bait similarity to another title | Own branding; no misleading SEO |

**Be inspired, don’t imitate.** Parody/inspiration edge cases may be taken down on legal request; marketing visibility may be limited.

**中文：** 艺术风格、核心循环、进度/经济、UI/音频、角色、名称/缩略图等维度：克隆 = 几乎相同；启发 = 有原创贡献。**受启发，勿模仿。** 法律请求可下架；营销曝光可能受限。

## Working With AI

**English:** Poki encourages thoughtful AI use. **Golden rule:** AI should **empower**, not **shortcut**—add value, not replace craft, quality, or originality.

**Before submit:** Clean consistent visuals; no AI watermarks/prompt text; review dialogue; **test gameplay**—no untested AI content.

**IP / process:** Solely AI-created games may raise IP ownership issues—be ready to document tools, prompts, and iteration on request. Prototyping with templates/AI is OK early; before **Player Fit** / **Web Fit**, the game must show unique perspective or meaningful improvement. Heavy AI/template reliance without personality, polish, or innovation may fail **Web Fit** or Final Review.

**中文：** 鼓励审慎使用 AI。**黄金法则：** AI **赋能**而非**走捷径**，须增值而非取代工艺与原创。

**提交前：** 视觉干净一致；无水印/提示词残留；审对话；**必须试玩**——禁未测 AI 内容。

**IP：** 纯 AI 作品可能有 IP 归属风险，须能说明工具与流程。早期原型可用模板/AI；进 **Player Fit / Web Fit** 前须有独特视角或实质改进。过度依赖 AI/模板且无个性/打磨/创新，可能在 **Web Fit** 或终审失败。

---

## Acceptable AI use (by asset type)

**English:**

| Area | ✅ Do | ❌ Don’t |
|------|-------|----------|
| **Thumbnails** | Match in-game style; accurate tone | Mismatched AI styles; misleading vs gameplay |
| **In-game visuals** | Heavy edit/polish; unified direction; concept → refine | Raw drop-in; mixed styles; watermarks; “Mario-style” prompts; AI won’t fix cloned concepts |
| **UI** | AI menus/icons if edited to match game | Raw paste; clashing UI; generic buttons |
| **Level design** | Procedural/AI assist if each level manually tested/balanced | Ship generated levels without design QA |
| **Narrative** | Brainstorm/drafts; human rewrite for tone | Unedited AI scripts in shipping build |
| **Code** | Copilot/ChatGPT for boilerplate; you own logic | Ship untested AI logic; skip balancing/QA |

**Credit:** Check each AI tool’s license (commercial use, attribution). Transparency appreciated for visibly AI art, AI text, or LLM-assisted code.

**中文：** 缩略图、局内视觉、UI、关卡、叙事、代码各有 Do/Don’t（见上表）。须查 AI 工具商用与署名条款；明显 AI 内容建议透明说明。

## Trends & Trend-Driven Content

**English:**

- ✅ Trends fuel **creative, fun** games.  
- ❌ Trends are **not** an excuse for rushed, low-effort, or repetitive content.

**What Poki looks for:** Enjoyable, polished experience even if some depth is sacrificed for speed; trend content must add **unique gameplay**, not meme/challenge collections without mechanics. May decline derivative games that don’t add diversity.

**Avoiding oversaturation:** Poki manages variety; crowded genres/trends may reject similar submissions. Approach trends from **fresh angles** that evolve the trend.

**Ongoing collaboration:** Poki works on sharing trend insights; welcomes developer feedback as trends evolve.

**中文：**

- ✅ 趋势应驱动**有创意、好玩**的游戏。  
- ❌ 不能成为粗制滥造、低投入、同质内容的借口。

**期望：** 即便为抢窗口牺牲部分深度，仍须可玩、有打磨；须有**独特玩法**，非纯梗/挑战堆砌。饱和品类可能拒相似稿；应从**新角度**发展趋势。平台会分享趋势洞察并欢迎反馈。

## Localization

**English:** More languages → more players. Especially important for text-heavy games on a global platform.

### Is localization right for your game?

**Strong candidates:** Story games, clicker/idle, quiz, any text-heavy or text-explained mechanics (guns, power-ups, etc.). Outside these, localization may have less ROI.

### Prepare for localization

Centralize translatable strings (e.g. one JSON file)—avoid hunting strings one-by-one later.

### Major language groups (priority order)

1. **EFIGS** — English, French, Italian, German, Spanish (+ consider Turkish).  
2. **CJK** — Simplified Chinese, Japanese, Korean (high cost; do if confident in ROI).  
3. **Brazilian Portuguese & Russian** — remaining global coverage.

### How to display languages

Simple menu toggle; **default:** serve browser language with a behind-the-scenes check.

**Requirements cross-ref:** Localization recommended for text-heavy games (see doc 03)—multiple UI languages; layouts must fit longer strings.

**中文：** 语言越多受众越广，**文本型**游戏尤其重要。

**适合本地化：** 剧情、点击/挂机、问答、靠文字说明机制的游戏；其他类型 ROI 可能较低。

**准备：** 文案集中（如 JSON），勿散落各处。

**语言优先级：** **EFIGS**（+ 可考虑土耳其语）→ **CJK** → **巴西葡语 & 俄语**。

**展示：** 菜单切换；**默认**按浏览器语言并在后台检测。

**与 Requirements 交叉：** 文本密集游戏建议多语言；布局适配更长译文（见 03）。
