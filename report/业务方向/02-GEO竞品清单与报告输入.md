## 一、需求与方向简述 / The Need & Direction

### 中文

- **需求**：越来越多用户直接问 ChatGPT、Perplexity、Google AI 概览，而不是翻搜索链接。品牌方看不见、也控制不了自己"在 AI 答案里的样子"——出现了吗？排第几？被哪个竞品盖过？AI 引用了谁？
- **方向（GEO / AI 可见度）**：一类工具，跨多个 AI 引擎批量问"购买意图"问题，测量并持续监测某品牌的出现率、排名、竞品对比、情绪、被引用来源，并给出改善建议。
- **付费逻辑**：跨引擎批量、按时间持续监测、可溯源报告、可执行建议——这些是无状态的免费 GPT 替代不了的。

### English

- **Need**: More users ask ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews directly instead of clicking search links. Brands can't see or control how they appear in AI answers — are they mentioned? ranked where? outshined by which competitor? which sources did the AI cite?
- **Direction (GEO / AI visibility)**: Tools that query buyer-intent prompts across multiple AI engines, then measure and continuously monitor a brand's mention rate, ranking, competitor comparison, sentiment, and cited sources — with actionable recommendations.
- **Why people pay**: cross-engine at scale, continuous time-series monitoring, traceable reports, and actionable advice — things a stateless free GPT cannot deliver.
