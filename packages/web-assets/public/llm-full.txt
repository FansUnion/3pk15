# Fangrush (三狼连猎)
URL: https://fangrush.com
Type: Free browser board game. No download. No account (v1).
Platform: Web (desktop + mobile).

## One-liner
EN: Command 3 wolves. Gap-rush the flock in chains — break lines with rocks.
ZH: 操控三狼，隔空连吃破阵，借岩石通关四季猎场。

## Rules (canonical)
- Board: 6x6 points, orthogonal moves only.
- Gap-eat: wolf — empty — sheep on a line; wolf lands on sheep; sheep removed.
- Chain eats: up to 5 per wolf turn; ending chain early allowed.
- Win: eat 8 sheep to clear. Lose: all three wolves have no legal moves.
- Sheep AI moves automatically; sheep cannot retreat toward row 1.
- Rocks block points; used for seasonal difficulty.

## Seasons
Production sheep AI profiles follow the learning curve: Spring guided/foundation, Summer tactical, Autumn strategic, and Winter expert. Legacy easy/normal/hard values are implementation compatibility tiers, not the player-facing curve.
Each level has a short unique blurb on its /hunt page.
